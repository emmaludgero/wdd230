Discover the beauty of our town!

Museum of Oscar Niemeyer

Explore the rich history and artistry at the Museum of Oscar Niemeyer. Immerse yourself in captivating exhibits and discover the cultural heritage of Curitiba.

Pope's Wood

Experience tranquility and natural beauty at Pope's Wood. Wander through lush greenery, admire majestic trees, and enjoy a peaceful escape from the city hustle.

Botanical Garden

Delight your senses at the Botanical Garden. Marvel at stunning plant collections, stroll through picturesque landscapes, and unwind in the beauty of nature.

Barigui Park

Embrace outdoor adventure at Barigui Park. Discover vast green spaces, engage in recreational activities, and create cherished memories with family and friends.

Tanguá Park

Surrender to serenity at Tanguá Park. Take in breathtaking vistas, meander along scenic trails, and rejuvenate your spirit in this urban oasis.

University of the Environment (Unilivre)

Ignite your passion for sustainability at Unilivre. Engage in eco-conscious learning, foster environmental stewardship, and be a catalyst for positive change.
