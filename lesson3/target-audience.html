Tanguá Park Audience Design

Target Audience

The target audience can be defined as follows

Nature Enthusiasts: This group includes individuals who have a deep appreciation for nature, biodiversity, and environmental conservation. They enjoy spending time outdoors, exploring parks, and engaging in nature-oriented activities. They are interested in learning about the flora, fauna, and ecological significance of Tanguá Park. They value immersive experiences and seek to connect with nature on a personal level.

Local Residents and Tourists: This group consists of local residents of Curitiba and tourists visiting the city. They are interested in exploring Tanguá Park as a recreational destination, taking part in various activities, and appreciating the park's natural beauty. They seek practical information about park facilities, operating hours, admission fees, and guided tours. They want to make the most of their visit to the park and may also be interested in learning about the historical and cultural significance of the park.

Environmental Educators and Professionals: This group includes individuals working in environmental fields, such as environmental scientists, educators, researchers, and conservationists. They seek in-depth information about Tanguá Park's biodiversity, ecological conservation efforts, and sustainability initiatives. They may use the website as a resource to gather data, study the park's ecosystem, and support their environmental education or research projects.

Personas

Persona 1:

Name: Natalie Green
Age: 32
Occupation: Environmental Scientist
Background: Natalie is an avid nature lover and is deeply passionate about environmental conservation. She spends her weekends exploring parks, hiking trails, and nature reserves. She is knowledgeable about various flora and fauna species and enjoys capturing photographs of natural landscapes. Natalie believes in the importance of sustainable tourism and wants to learn more about Tanguá Park's unique features and conservation efforts. She is interested in participating in eco-friendly activities and wants to contribute to the park's preservation.
Goals and Behaviors:
-> Natalie seeks detailed information about Tanguá Park's biodiversity and ecological significance.
-> She wants to engage in nature-oriented activities, such as bird watching and guided nature walks, during her visit.
-> Natalie is interested in attending workshops and programs that focus on environmental education and sustainability.
-> She appreciates virtual tours and photo galleries that allow her to immerse herself in the park's beauty before her visit.
-> Natalie values community engagement and would be eager to share her experiences and knowledge with fellow park enthusiasts.

Persona 2:

Name: Lucas Santos
Age: 26
Occupation: Tour Guide

Lucas wants to keep himself updated about upcoming events and programs in the park to offer an enhanced experience to his clients. -> Lucas wants to stay informed about any recent developments or changes in Tanguá Park to provide accurate information to his clients.
-> He looks for details about guided tours, park hours, and admission fees to plan his visits and tours effectively.

Scenario 1: Natalie's Research and Planning

Natalie, the nature enthusiast, is planning a trip to Tanguá Park. She visits the website to gather detailed information about the park's biodiversity, hiking trails, and recommended activities. She explores the virtual tours and photo galleries to get a sense of the park's beauty. She also checks the calendar of events to see if there are any upcoming workshops or programs aligned with her interests.

Scenario 2: Lucas's Tour Guide Preparation

Lucas, a local tour guide, wants to enhance his knowledge about Tanguá Park to offer an exceptional experience to his clients. He visits the website to stay updated on any recent changes or additions to the park's facilities. He checks the guided tour options and familiarizes himself with the park's historical significance to share captivating stories with his tour groups. He downloads park maps and safety guidelines to ensure a smooth and informative tour.

Scenario 3: Community Engagement

A group of visitors recently had a memorable experience at Tanguá Park. They want to share their photographs, stories, and recommendations with other park enthusiasts. They visit the website's community forum or blog to post their experiences, interact with other visitors, and offer tips to those planning a visit. They appreciate the opportunity to connect and engage with a community of like-minded individuals.

Scenario 4: Educators' Resource Gathering

A group of environmental educators is preparing a lesson plan on biodiversity conservation and wants to use Tanguá Park as a case study. They visit the website to access educational resources, such as downloadable fact sheets, research papers, and multimedia content that highlight the park's ecological importance. They gather data and insights to enrich their teaching materials and engage their students in meaningful discussions about environmental conservation.

Scenario 5: Virtual Exploration

Sarah, an individual unable to visit Tanguá Park physically due to geographical limitations or personal circumstances, wants to experience the park virtually. She visits the website and immerses herself in the interactive virtual tours, exploring different viewpoints, waterfalls, and lakes. She appreciates the detailed descriptions and high-quality photographs that allow her to appreciate the park's natural beauty from the comfort of her home. She may even use the website as inspiration for future travel plans.
