Typography

Inter and sans-serif

Inter, crafted by Rasmus Andersson, is a sans-serif font meticulously designed for screen use. It boasts a tall x-height, enhancing legibility across various sizes, and offers a diverse set of glyphs, weights, and special features. We adore its versatility. Moreover, Inter is both free and open source, eliminating the need for paid licenses or accreditation for use. You can freely download it from Google Fonts.

Sans-serif typography is characterized by fonts without decorative strokes, providing a clean and modern appearance which offers a clean and contemporary look, enhancing readability on digital screens.
